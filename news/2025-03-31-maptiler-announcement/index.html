    • MapTiler becomes a MapLibre Silver Sponsor

    Mar 31, 2025

    Categories: Announcements
    Authors: Ramya Ragupathy

    We are excited to announce that MapTiler has joined the MapLibre project as a Silver Sponsor!

    MapTiler is a global provider of mapping services and software tools, recognized for their strong commitment to the open-source mapping ecosystem. They are the original creators and primary maintainers of the OpenMapTiles project, and actively contribute to MapLibre, OSMNames, and several other open-source initiatives.

    MapTiler has played a significant role in the early development of MapLibre, contributing to the initial MapLibre Native SDKs, designing the logo and website, supporting fundraising, legal, operational and marketing efforts. Their contributions span both technical development and community-building.

    By becoming a Silver Sponsor, MapTiler continues its strong commitment to open-source geospatial software and to the growing MapLibre ecosystem.

    We’re grateful for MapTiler’s continued support and look forward to ongoing collaboration!
