    Donations by Individuals

    If you personally want to support MapLibre with a donation you have two options:

    • Either you can make a donation on GitHub: https://github.com/sponsors/maplibre/
    • Or you can make a donation on OpenCollective: https://opencollective.com/maplibre/

    Donations by Small Businesses

    If you own or work a small business and would like to support MapLibre financially but cannot justify joining the MapLibre Sponsorship Program, we recommend that you make a donation with a custom amount on OpenCollective.

    • Donation with custom amount: https://opencollective.com/maplibre/donate

    Fiscal Host and Accounting Transparency

    Open Source Collective 501(C)(6), a registered non-profit organization located in Covina, CA (USA), is MapLibre's fiscal host. Transactions from sponsors are directed to Open Source Collective, and all expenses are publicly visible with full accounting transparency at https://opencollective.com/maplibre/transactions.
