We are happy to announce the results of the 2025 MapLibre Governing Board election and also welcome our newest Voting Members. A big thanks to everyone who participated in this year's election process.
Your engagement is what keeps MapLibre a strong, community-driven project.

## Board Results

The following members have been elected to serve on the 2025 MapLibre Governing Board:

- [Yuri Astrakhan](https://github.com/maplibre/maplibre/discussions/445#discussioncomment-13864204) (incumbent)
- [Frank Elsinga](https://github.com/maplibre/maplibre/discussions/445#discussioncomment-14103318) (new)
- [Stephanie May](https://github.com/maplibre/maplibre/discussions/445#discussioncomment-14097777) (new)
- [Birk Skyum](https://github.com/maplibre/maplibre/discussions/445#discussioncomment-13754385) (incumbent)
- [Haowen You](https://github.com/maplibre/maplibre/discussions/445#discussioncomment-14074961) (incumbent)

## üìä Key Election Statistics

- Total eligible voters (Voting Members): 141
- Ballots cast: 92
- Voter turnout: ~65% (highest since 2022)
- Seats available: 5 (out of 6 candidates)
- Election method: Conducted online using [OpaVote](https://opavote.com/), with ranked-choice voting (Meek STV).
