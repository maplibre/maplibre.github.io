The MapLibre Governing Board is making a mid-term member change to remain in compliance with the [MapLibre Charter](https://github.com/maplibre/maplibre/blob/main/CHARTER.md).

## Why this change is happening

Frank Elsinga is joining Rivian / RV Tech as an intern. Yuri Astrakhan, a fellow board member, is also employed by Rivian / RV Tech. The Charter is explicit on this point:

> _No more than one person on the Governing Board shall be employed or contracted by a single Third-party Organization._

Rather than wait for the next election, Frank is stepping back from the board effective immediately so that the MapLibre Organization remains in full compliance.

## How the replacement works

The Charter defines a clear process for mid-term vacancies:

> _In the event of a Governing Board member resigning from the Board during a term, the nominees from the last election with the highest ranking shall be contacted with a proposal to serve in the Governing Board until the next election is called._

[Stefan Karschti](https://github.com/stefankarschti) was the next-highest-ranked candidate in the [2025 Governing Board election](/news/2025-09-22-board-elections-2025/) and has agreed to serve the remainder of the term.

## Welcome Stefan, and thank you Frank

We welcome [Stefan Karschti](/about/stefan-karschti) to the Governing Board. Stefan is a mapping systems engineer with over a decade of experience, including work on MapLibre Native's Metal backend and renderer rearchitecture. We look forward to his contributions.

We thank Frank for his work during his time on the board and wish him well at Rivian.

The updated board composition is reflected on the [About](/about) page.
