## 📱 MapLibre Native

- Thanks to the efforts of Adrian Cojocaru and Alex Cristici the [fill extrusion optimizations](https://github.com/maplibre/maplibre-native/pull/4310) that take advantage of instancing have been implemented for Vulkan as well. If you are using MapLibre Android, this is yet another reason to look into using the Vulkan backend (the default since 13.0.0).

- Vladyslav Odobesku created a [discussion thread](https://osmus.slack.com/archives/C04G140P9U6/p1781859352910629) on Slack about adding a Kotlin based `CustomVectorTileProvider` that would be a more performant alternative to `CustomGeometrySource`.

- Harel Mazor shared a [blog post](https://medium.com/@harel.mazor/putting-mapeak-on-the-dashboard-a6cc73d4f99e) with his experiences bringing adding support for Android Auto and Carplay to his app using MapLibre Native. He also published a project to bring contours to MapLibre iOS and Android using a Rust-based [HTTP interceptor](https://github.com/Mapeak-com/maplibre-contour-rs).

- The [MapLibre Native FFI](https://maplibre.org/maplibre-native-ffi) project now has browser support using the WebGPU backend. See the [discussion thread on Slack](https://osmus.slack.com/archives/C02B2CBSNBU/p1782547722335569?thread_ts=1778119369.635259&cid=C02B2CBSNBU) and [the relevant PR](https://github.com/maplibre/maplibre-native-ffi/pull/285) for more details.

- Taku Fukada from our valued sponsor MIERUNE implemented [double resolution rendering of CJK characters](https://github.com/maplibre/maplibre-native/pull/4304). This makes these characters much more legible. MapLibre GL JS has had this behavior for a while already.

- Releases.
  - MapLibre Android [13.3.0](https://github.com/maplibre/maplibre-native/releases/tag/android-v13.3.0), [13.3.1](https://github.com/maplibre/maplibre-native/releases/tag/android-v13.3.1)
  - MapLibre iOS [6.27.0](https://github.com/maplibre/maplibre-native/releases/tag/ios-v6.27.0)
