MLT 1.0 is stable and recommended for production.
Several extensions are being designed and prototyped that may land as a future MLT release.

Themes under exploration:

- **3D support** - 3D coordinates and elevation for 2.5D and 3D basemaps
- **Nested types** - Lists, maps, and nested properties to support modern source formats such as Overture (GeoParquet)
- **Smaller metadata** - Reduce per-tile header overhead and share encoding metadata across columns
