    Financial Reports

    MapLibre uses OpenCollective for transparent accounting, see https://opencollective.com/maplibre. Below you find quarterly financial reports which break the transactions down into categories. The MapLibre Sponsorship Program allocates a fraction of larger donations to bounties. This is why we keep track of two accounts: the General account and the Bounty account.

    Open Source Collective, a 501(c)(6) registered non-profit organization based in Covina, CA (USA), is MapLibre’s fiscal host.
