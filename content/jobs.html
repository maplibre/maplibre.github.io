Jobs

The MapLibre Organization is looking for people who would like to support the community on a paid basis. If you are interested in working on open-source mapping libraries and helping the community to get the most out of their contributions, MapLibre could be a great place for you.

We have three different roles: Coordinator, Maintainer, and Developer. The Coordinator focuses on organizational work, Maintainers take care of a GitHub repository, and Developers work on Bounties. You find more information about the paid roles in our wiki on GitHub.

To be able to hire people worldwide, we require the applicants to be freelancers who take care of income taxes etc themselves and send the MapLibre Organization an invoice for their work.

The Coordinator and Maintainers track their time and work on an agreed hourly rate. Please indicate what your hourly rate would be and how many hours per week you would be available.

The roles are open to part-time work, so if you for example take care of your family and would like to work one hour per day on open-source, then it is completely fine to apply with an availability of a few hours per week.

We understand that everyone's life is different and will consider all applications independent of a person's background, gender, education, or nationality.

The MapLibre Organization is looking forward to your application!
